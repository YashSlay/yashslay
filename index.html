CHARACTERS OF NOLI ME TANGERE




Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

Maria Clara

Don Rafael Ibarra

Kapitan Tiago
An exceptionally opulent young man. He studied abroad in Europe and is treated highly with great sophistication and respect by the friars and priests. Notably a beautiful young woman. Is described to be "the star of a dark night." Better known as the female lead or second main character of Noli Me Tangere. The father of Crisostomo Ibarra. Other opposing affluent figures gravely envied him. He had many political competitors and they seek to bring him down.
A two-faced barangay captain who is very wealthy and is seen as a remarkable man in the eyes of the fathers. Outwardly delegates his work to showcase his good deeds, but inwardly conceals his scandalous activities. The host of the event held on "Kalyeng Anulwage."
